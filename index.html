About Us

Rakam Blue Cyber is a modern cyber café and internet solutions center based in Kenya, established with the goal of providing reliable, fast, and affordable digital services to individuals, students, and businesses.

Since opening three months ago, Rakam Blue Cyber has been driven by a team of dedicated and hardworking personnel committed to delivering quality service and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Our cyber offers a wide range of services including high-speed internet access, printing, photocopying, document processing, online applications, and various internet solutions.
